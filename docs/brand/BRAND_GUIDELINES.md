## 01 — Brand Story & Positioning

**Architects of Ambition.**

Mabishion is a business-engineering studio — part AI digital-marketing agency, part operating system for building ventures.

We don't just execute campaigns — we architect the systems, teams and technology that let ambition compound. Every engagement is treated like a blueprint: measured, structured and built to last.

At the centre sits **Mabishion AI Studio**, our private business engine, and **Mickii** — an AI agent orchestrating a workforce of specialist workers behind approval gates, so the founder stays in command while the machine does the work.

The identity carries this dual nature: the disciplined *navy* of an architect's drafting ink, and the *gold* of ambition realised. Classic where it counts, engineered where it matters.

- **Mission** — To engineer the systems that turn ambition into enterprise.
- **Promise** — Founder in command. Machine at work. Nothing ships without approval.
- **Personality** — Disciplined, warm, direct. An architect who speaks your language.

## 02 — The Logo System

**The Mark.** The primary lockup pairs the gold chevron **M** — two triangles meeting at a central peak, drawn like a roofline — with the Marcellus wordmark and the tagline. This is the default expression of the brand.

- **Primary lockup:** Navy `#243B4A` ground · gold mark · Marcellus caps at 0.20em tracking.

### Lockup variants
- Horizontal (mark + wordmark)
- Light background (navy mark + navy wordmark)
- Mark only
- Gold background (navy mark + wordmark)
- One-colour navy
- App icon / avatar (rounded navy tile)

### Clear space & sizing
- **Clear space:** keep clear space equal to the **full height of the mark** on all sides. Nothing should intrude on this zone.
- **Minimum size:** mark **24px**; full lockup **120px** wide.

### Misuse — never
- Stretch, skew or distort the mark.
- Recolour outside the palette.
- Place on low-contrast grounds.
- Add shadows or effects.
- Substitute the typeface.
- Tighten the letter-spacing.

## 03 — Colour

Navy leads, gold accents, cream grounds. Navy should dominate primary surfaces; gold is reserved for the mark, accents and moments of emphasis — never large fields.

**Core palette**
- Navy — `#243B4A`
- Navy Deep — `#1B2E3A`
- Gold — `#C9A24B`
- Gold Deep — `#A9823A`
- Cream — `#EDE7DD`

**Supporting neutrals**
- Slate Blue — `#9BB0BC`
- Paper — `#F4F1EA`

**Usage balance:** roughly **60% Navy / 30% Cream / 10% Gold**. Gold earns its impact by staying scarce.

## 04 — Typography

**Marcellus** — Display & Headings. Used for the wordmark, headlines and pull-quotes. Set headings in **caps with 0.10–0.20em tracking**. A single serif carries the whole hierarchy — vary size, not weight.

**Jost** — Body & UI (weights 300 / 400 / 500 / 600). Used for body copy, captions, UI and the tagline. Taglines and micro-labels set in **uppercase at 0.28–0.30em tracking**. Body copy stays sentence case, 300–400 weight.

**Hierarchy in use**
- Eyebrow — Jost 11px / 0.28em uppercase
- Headline — Marcellus, ~34px, 0.03em
- Body — Jost regular, 55–65 character measure, line-height ~1.7, sentence case

## 05 — Iconography & Graphic Language

A single line-art set: **24px grid, 1.9px stroke, round caps and joins, no fill**. Icons render in navy on light grounds, cream on dark. Keep them geometric and calm — they support, never shout.

**The chevron motif:** the mark's twin-triangle peak is the brand's core shape. Use it as a divider, a bullet, or a large tonal watermark on covers — always at the same 30° pitch as the logo.

## 06 — Voice & Tone

The voice is **Hinglish** — Hindi warmth over English precision. Direct, founder-personal and confident, with the occasional emoji for punctuation. It sounds like a trusted operator, not a corporation.

- **Warm** — speaks to the founder by name. Personal, never stiff.
- **Direct** — short sentences. Say the thing. No corporate padding.
- **In command** — confident and reassuring. The machine works; you decide.

**Hinglish in practice**
- "Mickii se poocho — website banao, campaign chalao, ya deal close karo."
- "Koi pending approval nahi. ✅"
- "Welcome back, Ashu. 🌅 Aaj kya build karein?"
- "Apna PIN daalo — access ke liye. 🔐"

**Do**
- "Deal ho gaya! 3 naye leads add kiye."
- Mix languages naturally, mid-sentence.
- Keep micro-labels in crisp English caps.

**Don't**
- "We are pleased to inform you that…"
- Force Hindi where it feels unnatural.
- Overload with emoji — one per thought, max.

## 07 — Product UI Theme — Mabishion AI Studio

> **Owner Decision — 2026-07-16.** This section previously specified an indigo/slate sub-theme
> with Inter type. That was never built and is now retired: the app carries the **master brand**
> — navy grounds, gold accents, Marcellus + Jost — so the identity is one system end to end,
> not two. The indigo palette below survives only as **status/data-viz accents**.

The software wears a darker, engineered skin — a deep navy canvas with glass surfaces, gold
accents and Marcellus display type. It is the master brand at night, not a separate theme.

**App palette (source of truth: `src/components/consts.js`)**
- Canvas / glass base — Navy Deep `#1B2E3A`
- Surface — Navy `#243B4A`
- Accent — Gold `#C9A24B` (scarce: mark, emphasis, primary actions)
- Text on dark — Cream `#EDE7DD`
- Muted — Slate Blue `#9BB0BC`

**Status & data-viz accents only** (never brand surfaces): Success `#10B981` · Danger `#EF4444` ·
Info `#3B82F6` · Indigo `#6366F1` · Violet `#8B5CF6`.

**Glass surfaces:** `linear-gradient(145deg, rgba(255,255,255,.05), rgba(255,255,255,.02))` over
the navy base, 1px `rgba(255,255,255,.1)` border, 25px blur. Radius 14–24px (app uses 18px).

**Type in product:** Marcellus for headlines and stat values; Jost for body, UI and micro-labels.
Eyebrows/labels/tabs/badges: uppercase at `0.28em` (`tracking-eyebrow`). Headline: Marcellus 34px
at `0.03em`. Body: `line-height 1.7`, 55–65 character measure.
