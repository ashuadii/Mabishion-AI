## Introduction

### Purpose
Mabishion exists to close the gap between ambition and resources. Every growing business hits the same wall: agencies are expensive and slow, freelancers are inconsistent, and DIY tools produce amateur results. Mabishion removes that wall.

We are an AI-powered digital factory that delivers four things exceptionally well — digital marketing, website development, custom software, and AI development. Artificial intelligence does the heavy production work; a human reviews and approves every critical output before it reaches a client. The result: agency-grade quality at a fraction of agency cost and time.

Mabishion is not built to serve everyone. It is built for ambitious business owners — people who treat their business as a mission, want a partner who moves as fast as they think, and refuse to let budget decide the quality of their brand.

### About the brand
Mabishion began as one founder's answer to a broken trade-off: small businesses either overpay agencies or settle for amateur work. Built as a private AI-powered digital factory, Mabishion combines a marketer's eye with an engineer's discipline — AI does the heavy lifting, a human makes every final call. The name fuses **M** (the mark), **ambition**, and **vision** — because the brand exists to build for people whose ambition outruns their resources.

### Our mission
To give ambitious business owners agency-grade digital work — marketing, websites, software, and AI — delivered fast, priced fairly, and finished with obsessive attention to detail.

### Our vision
A world where a business's size never limits its ambition — where one determined founder, armed with the right intelligence, can outbuild a full agency.

## Logo

### Primary logo
- **The shape:** A geometric "M" monogram — clean, symmetrical strokes forming a modern emblem that reads equally well at favicon size and on a hoarding.
- **The symbol:** The twin peaks of the M represent ambition (the climb) and delivery (the summit). Paired with the wordmark MABISHION and the tagline "Architects of Ambition."
- **The style:** Minimal, luxurious, confident. Gold mark on deep navy is the hero treatment.

### Secondary logo / variations
- **Gold mark (`mabishion-mark-gold`):** Gold (#C9A24B) monogram. Use on navy or dark backgrounds — app interfaces, dark social creatives, premium documents.
- **Navy mark (`mabishion-mark-navy`):** Navy (#243B4A) monogram. Use on cream (#F4F1EA) or white backgrounds — letterheads, invoices, light-theme documents.
- **Icon-only mark (`mabishion-mark`):** Use where space is tight — favicons, app icons, social avatars, watermarks.

### Logo usage
- **Clear space:** Keep empty space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" mark on all sides. Nothing enters this zone — no text, no graphics, no edges.
- **Minimum size:** 24 px height digital; 10 mm height print. Below this, use the icon-only mark.
- **Never:** stretch, rotate, recolor outside the approved palette, add drop shadows, or place the gold mark on light backgrounds (poor contrast — use navy mark instead).

## Typography

### Fonts
| Role | Font | Weights | Use case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Headings** | Marcellus (serif) | Regular (single weight) | Titles, headlines, big numbers, section headings — the "luxury voice" |
| **Body** | Jost (sans-serif) | 400 / 500 / 600 / 700 / 800 | Body copy, UI, captions, labels, buttons |

Fallbacks: Marcellus → Georgia, serif. Jost → Segoe UI, system-ui, sans-serif.
Both are free Google Fonts (₹0 licensing, print + digital).

### Brand tagline lockup
**Architects of Ambition** — set in Jost, uppercase, letter-spaced wide (tracking +0.15 em), under the MABISHION wordmark.

### Typesetting and hierarchy
| Style | Font | Size |
|---|---|---|
| Title | Marcellus | 60 pt |
| Heading | Marcellus | 32 pt |
| Subtitle | Marcellus | 24 pt |
| Section Header | Jost Bold, uppercase, wide tracking | 16 pt |
| Subheading | Jost SemiBold | 12 pt |
| Body | Jost Regular | 12 pt |
| Quote | Marcellus, italic feel via spacing | 15 pt |
| Caption | Jost Medium | 9 pt |

Rules: generous line-height (1.5 body, 1.2 headings); never set Marcellus in bold (it has one weight — scale it instead); uppercase + wide tracking is reserved for small labels and section headers.

## Voice

### Brand voice
**Ambitious** — We talk about outcomes, not features. Every line points at growth.
*"Your business isn't small. It's early."*

**Straightforward** — No jargon, no fluff, no fake urgency. We say the price, the timeline, and the truth.
*"Here's what it costs, here's when it ships, here's exactly what you get."*

**Warm** — We write like a trusted partner, not a vendor. Clients are people with names, not "leads."
*"Aapka business, hamari zimmedari — let's build something you're proud of."*

**Premium-minimal** — Fewer words, better words. White space is part of the voice.
*"Built. Delivered. Done."*

### Tone variations
| Channel | Tone | Sample copy |
|---|---|---|
| Client proposals & documents | Formal, precise, professional English | "This proposal outlines scope, timeline, and investment for your project. Every deliverable listed is included in the quoted price." |
| WhatsApp (clients) | Warm, personal, light Hinglish | "Namaste! Aapki website ka first preview ready hai — link neeche hai. Feedback bataiye, changes hum turant kar denge. 🙏" |
| Instagram / LinkedIn | Bold, aspirational, punchy | "Agencies quoted them ₹2 lakh. We shipped it in 10 days. Ambition doesn't wait — neither do we." |
| Email | Clear, courteous, structured | "Hi Priya, sharing your project update: design is approved, development completes Thursday, and delivery is on track for Monday." |
| Website | Confident, benefit-led | "Agency-grade websites, marketing, and AI — built for businesses that refuse to stay small." |

## Photography

### General guidelines
Real over staged. Warm, natural light with deep shadows — imagery should feel like the brand palette (navy depth, gold warmth, cream calm). Editing: slightly lifted blacks, warm temperature, muted saturation; no heavy filters, no HDR, no watermarked stock, and never generic Western corporate stock — our world is Indian businesses, real desks, real shops.

- **People:** Founders and owners at work — candid, dignified, in their real environment. Eye-level angles. No fake handshake photos.
- **Products:** Screens and deliverables shown in context (laptop on a real desk, phone in a real hand). Dark-mode app screenshots framed on navy backgrounds with gold accents.
- **Places:** Indian streets, markets, workshops, offices — shot warm and honest. Architecture and city energy that mirrors "building" and "ambition."

## Graphics
- **Style:** Flat, geometric, minimal — echoing the M mark's clean strokes. Thin gold line-work on navy; no gradients except subtle navy-to-ink washes.
- **Line weight:** Consistent 1.5–2 px strokes at standard sizes.
- **Color application:** Navy bases, gold accents, cream negative space. Shading via flat tonal steps, not soft shadows.
- **Motion (if used):** Calm and precise — fades and slides, 200–300 ms, no bounces.

## Icons
- **Style:** Outline icons, rounded terminals, consistent 1.5–2 px stroke (matches the app's icon set).
- **Sizes:** 12 / 16 / 24 px grid with equal padding; never scale optically mismatched sizes together.
- **Color:** Single-color only — gold or muted slate on dark, navy on light. Never multicolor icons.
- **With text:** Icon inherits text color; gap of 8 px; vertically centered.

## Charts
Consistent with the app's Reports screen (Recharts):
- **Bar chart:** Comparing categories — revenue by month, leads by source. Gold bars on navy, cream gridlines at 10% opacity.
- **Pie chart:** Composition only, max 5 slices — gold + navy + slate tones; avoid when categories exceed 5 (use bars).
- **Line chart:** Trends over time — pipeline growth, cost tracking. Gold primary line, info-blue secondary, soft area fill under primary.
Rules: no 3D, no rainbow palettes, label directly where possible, and always state the time period.
